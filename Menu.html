what's make our coffee special!

Quality of Beans: The type and quality of coffee beans used are crucial. Arabica beans, for example, are often considered superior to Robusta due to their milder flavor and aroma. Additionally, beans from specific regions or estates can have unique flavor profiles. Roasting: The roasting process greatly affects the taste of coffee. Light roasts have a different flavor profile than dark roasts. Specialized roasting techniques can bring out specific flavors and aromas in the beans. Freshness: Freshly roasted and ground coffee beans make a big difference. Coffee is at its best shortly after roasting, so buying freshly roasted beans and grinding them just before brewing can enhance the flavor. Brewing Method: The method used to brew coffee can greatly influence its taste. Options like French press, pour-over, espresso, or cold brew all yield different results. Each method can bring out different flavors and aromas in the coffee.

It's a popular choice, especially during warm weather, for those who prefer their coffee cold or want a refreshing coffee option. Cappuccino

A cappuccino is a classic espresso-based coffee beverage known for its rich and creamy texture. It's made by combining espresso with steamed milk and milk foam. The name "cappuccino" is derived from the Capuchin friars' robes, which the drink's color is said to resemble due to its combination of dark coffee and white foam.

$8.99

Mocha

A mocha, short for "mocha coffee" or "mocha latte," is a delightful coffee beverage that combines the flavors of espresso, chocolate, and steamed milk. It's a popular choice for those who enjoy the rich, sweet, and slightly bitter notes of both coffee and chocolate.

$8.99

Espresso

Espresso is a concentrated coffee beverage that's made by forcing hot water through finely-ground coffee beans. It's known for its strong flavor, rich aroma, and the layer of crema that forms on top. Espresso is the base for many other coffee drinks, and it's enjoyed by coffee enthusiasts around the world.

$8.99

Ramen

Ramen is a popular Japanese noodle dish known for its flavorful broth, tender noodles, and various toppings. It has become a global comfort food with many regional and international variations. Ramen is not only a beloved comfort food in Japan but has gained popularity worldwide, with ramen shops and restaurants serving diverse and innovative ramen dishes to cater to different tastes and preferences.

Carbonara pasta

Carbonara pasta is a classic Italian pasta dish known for its creamy and flavorful sauce. It's a simple yet indulgent recipe traditionally made with a few key ingredients: pasta, eggs, cheese (usually Pecorino Romano or Parmesan), guanciale (an Italian cured pork cheek), and black pepper.

French toast

French toast, also known as "eggy bread" or "pain perdu" in French, is a delicious breakfast dish made from slices of bread soaked in a mixture of beaten eggs and milk, then fried until golden brown. It's typically served with a variety of toppings and condiments, such as maple syrup, powdered sugar, fresh fruit, whipped cream, or even savory options like bacon or ham.
